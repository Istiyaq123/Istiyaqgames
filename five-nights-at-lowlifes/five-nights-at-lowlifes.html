Five Nights at Lowlife's

Five Nights at Lowlife's is a fan-made game of the Scott Cawthon hugely big game Five Nights at Freddy's

Five Nights at Lowlife's takes place in a museum, where you will have to survive 6 hours from 12pm to 6am for 5 nights. You think you are safe, but really your not. Monsters are coming after you.

Watch the lights. Close the door. Check the camera. This night might be your last.
